This is NOT an official site

This site made by a couple passionate football fans who can not wait for the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia to commence and just wanted to have a simple app that would constantly show how much longer you have to wait until all the fun starts.

We do indeed also hope, that others might find some benefit from it and share their anxiety as we wait for the greatest sporting event in the world to begin.

For any questions, requests, suggestions or even complaints; please don't hesitate to send us an email. We'll try to get back to you as soon as possilbe.
