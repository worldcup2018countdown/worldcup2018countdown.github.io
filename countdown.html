Share Tips

TL;DR : Pause > Fullscreen > Screenshot > Paste

On desktops

  • Windows

    1. Click the Freeze button on the countdown to pause the countdown.
    2. Click the Fullscreen button on the countdown page.
    3. Press Alt + Print Screen to automatically screenshot and save the image to clipboard.
    4. Click the Normalscreen to remove fullscreen mode.
    5. Press Ctrl + V on message or post inbox of a social media of your chosing to upload the image.

  • Mac OS

    1. Click the Freeze button on the countdown to pause the countdown.
    2. Click the Fullscreen button on the countdown page.
    3. Press Shift + Ctrl + Cmd + 3 to automatically screenshot and save the image to clipboard.
    4. Click the Normalscreen to remove fullscreen mode.
    5. Press Cmd + V on message or post inbox of a social media of your chosing to upload the image.

On mobile and tablet devices

  1. Click the Freeze button on the countdown to pause the countdown animations so as to ensure a clear and sharp countdown screenshot.
  2. Click the Fullscreen button on the countdown page.
  3. Take a screenshot using your device's screenshot shortcut.
  4. Click the Normalscreen to remove fullscreen mode.
  5. Upload the screenshot image to your favourite social media site.
